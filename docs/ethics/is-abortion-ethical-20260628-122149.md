**Original Question:**  
Mahodaya, you mentioned about abortion to deselect people who might be physically damaged. What is the ethics of abortion according to yoga? I think you mentioned once that a mind and jivatma are associated with the new life at conception. That is the reason I was wondering.

**Full Answer:**  
This is not a one-line answer.
- I mentioned it in today's session just to show that even in today's society there are the beginnings of acceptance of some form of selection or approach to trying to limit severe genetic malformations. But this current approach is not the one supported by yoga.

The ethics of abortion according to yoga

- Approach is determined by whether we are speaking about today's society or the ideal society which we are working to build.
- In today's society there are the stark realities of the devastating financial and emotional burden on families needing to care for a child with severe congenital abnormalities. It becomes unjust to prohibit abortions for such situations in today's world. 
- In an ideal society in which the whole societal structure is set up in such a fashion that children who are severely compromised at birth are cared for properly by the society, there abortion will not be permitted as the child is a living being who has the right to live as any other human does and thereby to continue its spiritual journey in srsti cakra.
