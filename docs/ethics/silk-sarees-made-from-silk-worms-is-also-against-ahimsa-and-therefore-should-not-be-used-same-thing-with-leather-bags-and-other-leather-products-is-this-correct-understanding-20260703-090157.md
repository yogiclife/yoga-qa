**Original Question:**  
Silk sarees made from silk worms is also against ahimsa and therefore should not be used? Same thing with leather bags and other leather products.  Is this correct understanding?

**Full Answer:**  
The point of ahimsa is more relevant for the more developed beings, so for example leather goods should definitely be avoided completely. There is no need for such products. Re silk, yes, better to avoid -- not as critical as with leather, as the worms are undeveloped beings. But better to avoid.
