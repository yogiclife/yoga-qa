**Original Question:**  
based on my day to day experience. While doing a focused activity, say nama japa or scripture reading, Initially I feel I am entirely into it, later I could feel my mind has slipped far away into something else, and the lips are doing a mechanical chore of chanting and suddenly  I realize the mind has wandered away, and then something brings back the mind to the activity of chanting again. So its like a hide and seek game that's going on during my prayer session. now I am trying to figure out the 3 molecules of the mind in this pakkadam pakkadai game. How to stay focused, please advise.”

**Full Answer:**  
The mind is a tool. To use the tool effectively, one needs training in how to use the tool properly. That training one gets in yoga. By following the yogic lifestyle, the mind gets trained in how to work properly.
