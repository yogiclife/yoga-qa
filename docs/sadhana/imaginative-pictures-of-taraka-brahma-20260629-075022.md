**Original Question:**  
Tarak Brahma in the form of shiva and Krishna appeared in this jagat but how far the so called pictures of shiva and Krishna shown in Indian mythology are linked with the Tarak Brahma. In one of your class you said the so called blue boy with flute is an artist’s imagination. Is it the same for the pictures of shiva?

**Full Answer:**  
Indeed, all the paintings, statues, and other images of Lord Shiva and Lord Krsna in the society today are artists' imagination. As there were no cameras back in the days when Lord Shiva and Lord Krsna walked this earth physically, people over the ages have heard about Lord Shiva and Lord Krsna but do not know what they looked like. As such, the artists also do not know. So, hearing the stories and tales, artists imagine what they think they might have looked like and create these images.
