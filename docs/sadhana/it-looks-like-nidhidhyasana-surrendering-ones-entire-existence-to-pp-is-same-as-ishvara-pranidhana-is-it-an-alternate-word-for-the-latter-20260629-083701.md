**Original Question:**  
it looks like nidhidhyasana -- surrendering one's entire existence to PP -- is same as ishvara pranidhana. Is it an alternate word for the latter?

**Full Answer:**  
Iishvara Pranidhana refers to the formal process of sadhana. Yes, it means taking the shelter of PP,  through the process of sadhana. Whereas, nidhidhyasana is the overall outlook or bhava of surrender to PP throughout the 24 hours, in all one's activities of the day.
