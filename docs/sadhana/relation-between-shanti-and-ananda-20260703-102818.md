**Original Question:**  
Relation between Shanti and Ananda.

**Full Answer:**  
There are two types of shanti: (1) parama shanti and (2) laokika shanti.

Parama shanti is achieved only through sadhana, and this parama shanti and ananda are one and the same thing. This is the state of samadhi (during life) and mukti/moksha (when the mind leaves the body).

Then there is laokika manasika shanti, which is what that discourse was about. This shanti is very important for proper functioning of the mind and body, but is a different sort of shanti from parama shanti. One is manasika shanti in the mundane world; the other is parama shanti achieved by moving ahead in sadhana and becoming one with Parama Purusa.
