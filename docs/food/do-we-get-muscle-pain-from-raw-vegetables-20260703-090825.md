**Original Question:**  
One bhagini was saying that she gets muscle pain eating raw vegetables. I don't think there is any connection between the two but wanted to hear your thoughts on thi

**Full Answer:**  
- Bhagini is not getting muscle pain from eating raw vegetables. She may be getting muscle pain from not eating enough raw vegetables, but she is not getting muscle pain from eating raw vegetables 😊
- Raw vegetables are the heathiest thing on the planet. In India there is some sort of huge dogma surrounding raw vegetables. I have been hearing this sort of thing for years from people in India. Not sure where this belief arose, but it seems to exist throughout India and certainly not for any good reason. One of the biggest problems is that, aside from a very few vegetables such as cucumbers, people never eat vegetables raw; hence, they have no idea what raw fresh vegetables taste like, or how good it feels to eat them. This needs to be changed. People in the USA may not eat a lot of raw vegetables, but the reason for this is not that they think they will get food poisoning from them or that they will give them muscle pains. They just aren't used to it, due in large part to the miseducation from the fast-food industry.
