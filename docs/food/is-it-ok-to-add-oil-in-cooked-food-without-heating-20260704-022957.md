**Original Question:**  
Is it OK to add oil in cooked food without heating?

**Full Answer:**  
Some years back I used to tell people that if they want they can add room-temperature oil to their meal after the meal is cooked, rather than heating the oil to high temperatures. But I have stopped saying that, as oil is not healthy at any temperature. Rather, it is quite harmful at any temperature. The best is to not include any oil at all.
