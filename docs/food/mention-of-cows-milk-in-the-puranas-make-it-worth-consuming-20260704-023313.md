**Original Question:**  
Several people asked about mention of cows milk in the puranas, and the stories about ancient rsis and munis keeping cows and using cows milk in their diet.

**Full Answer:**  
shok ji's explanation of this was well done: B12 is indeed needed and this was the source of B12 in ancient times. But in those days cow's milk and its products was taken only sparingly. Otherwise, if taken in large amounts then it would have been harmful then as it is now-- obesity, HLD, atherosclerosis, diabetes, etc. Now that we have vegan B12 supplements, we have solved the problem. The only reason for taking cows milk was for the B12. Now we can get B12 without the harmful effects of cows milk so today there is no reason at all to take cow's milk.
