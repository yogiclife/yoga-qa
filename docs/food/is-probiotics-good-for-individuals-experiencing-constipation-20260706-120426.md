**Original Question:**  
 Nowadays doctors seem to suggest probiotics for people suffering constipation. What effect it has on microbiome and what could be the side effects of probiotics..? 



**Full Answer:**  
The fresh foods that Swarup mahodaya recommends are prebiotic, nourishing the helpful microbes. So, we probably don’t need probiotics. That my understanding. [Ashok]

Regarding your second question, Ashok Mahodaya has provided the answer. Someone who eats a whole-food, plant-based diet with plenty of raw fruits and vegetables will (1) never be constipated, and (2) will have a wonderfully healthy microbiome. For such people there is absolutely no need to consume probiotics. And I really do not consider probiotics as a product to be any sort of solution to these issues. The solution is a whole-food, plant-based diet with plenty of raw fruits and vegetables.
