As you know, for the past six months I've been eating only raw, organic fruits and vegetables (except for during the yoga shivir). Today I received my most recent order of fruits and veggies. Here is what I received, all locally grown except for the mango (Mexico), oranges (Florida), kiwi (California):

1. Organic Empire Apples 40 lb (18 kg)
2. Organic EverCrisp Apples 40 lb (18 kg)
3. Organic Cortland Applies 40 lb (18 kg)
4. Organic Kiwi 90 count
5. Organic Mango 12 count
6. Organic Oranges 90 count
7. Organic Sweet Baby Broccoli 18 count
8. Organic Lacinato Kale (Bunches of large green leaves) 42 bunches count. (This is a huge amount-- two large boxes)
9. Organic Curly Parsley (herb) 30 bunches count
10. Organic Cilantro Delfino (herb) 1/4 lb
11. Organic Fennel Bulb 12 count
12. Organic Baby Arugula (leaves) 3 lb

Everything is grown on small organic farms, which makes it much much better than what one can purchase at even organic supermarkets in the US, as the supermarkets purchase from huge farms which breed their produce for shelf life. The local farms I purchase from still grow fruits and vegetables they way they are supposed to be, very natural, very tasty, and extremely fresh (picked the day before delivery). So beautiful! No cooking needed, no spices needed, nothing needed. All of it just ready to eat as it is!

I got 54 kg of apples, which is a huge amount.  They have a long life in the refrigerator, and I'll be eating a lot of them every day. Regarding fruit, I purchased mostly apples this time as it is winter time here, and apples are the main fruit which can grow in the cold weather here. Everything mentioned above will be finished in 4-5 weeks, and ready for the next order!
