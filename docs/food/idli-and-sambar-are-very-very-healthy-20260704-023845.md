**Original Question:**  
idli and sambar are very very healthy?

**Full Answer:**  
It is true that among the traditional foods, idli is far better as there is no oil and no frying involved. As a result, idli does not cause the harm which so many other traditional Indian dishes cause. There is salt in it, but less than in other dishes, and it can certainly be made without salt. But as for health supporting, it is highly processed, refined food.
