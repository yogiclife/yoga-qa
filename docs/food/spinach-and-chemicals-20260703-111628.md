**Original Question:**  
Regarding the question about spinach and chemical fertilizers

**Full Answer:**  
Many people ask about chemical fertilizers. The moment they hear that we suggest eating raw leafy greens, they start saying that raw vegetables are dangerous and cooked vegetables are safe. But the real fact is that in India, people simply do not want to eat vegetables without cooking them; they think it is tasteless. And they also think that cooking the vegetables will remove or destroy the chemical fertilizers. People should not think that cooking leafy greens will get rid of chemical fertilizers. Moreover, eating raw—meaning living—leafy greens is absolutely essential. So, what is XX ji trying to say about leafy greens for such a long time regarding this matter? She kept saying repeatedly that without chemical fertilizers, spinach won't grow at all. Is there no such thing as organic spinach? Of course it exists, and it grows abundantly.
