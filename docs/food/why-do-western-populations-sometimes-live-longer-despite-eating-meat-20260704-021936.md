**Original Question:**  
Why do Western populations sometimes live longer despite eating meat?

**Full Answer:**  
one huge factor is socio-economic level. One student was asking why people in the West live longer despite eating meat. Climate control is a big factor-- A/C in the summer, heat in the water. And other such conveniences have a big effect on health. Many people in India are very poor and may not get the treatment they need when sick. In the hot season in India, many people die just from the heat. So even if people in the US have a bad lifestyle, they get medical treatment and live in a climate-controlled environment and so somehow survive. But it is not that they are healthy.
