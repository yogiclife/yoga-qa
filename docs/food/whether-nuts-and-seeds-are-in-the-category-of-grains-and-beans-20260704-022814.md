**Original Question:**  
Whether nuts and seeds are in the category of grains and beans.

**Full Answer:**  
The answer is no: grains are their own category, beans are their own category, and nuts and seeds are an entirely different category. The reason for this distinction is that grains and beans are both 80-10-10 whereas nuts and seeds are not. Both nuts and seeds are mostly fat. Most nuts and seeds derive approximately 73% to 90% of their total calories from fat. Hence the separate classification.
