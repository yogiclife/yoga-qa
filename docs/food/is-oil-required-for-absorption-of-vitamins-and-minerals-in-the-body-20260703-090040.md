**Original Question:**  
Is oil required for absorption of vitamins and minerals in the body?

**Full Answer:**  
Oil: yes, required for those vitamins which are in the category of fat-soluble vitamins -- A, E, K, D. The requisite amounts of oil are contained in the foods which provide those vitamins. Vit D3 = from sunlight, good to take as a vegan supplement. Good to take together with a few nuts for example, which will facilitate its absorption. (No bottled oil is needed or recommended)
