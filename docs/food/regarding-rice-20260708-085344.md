**Full Answer:**  
What it really does is fill the stomach and take the place of far more health-supporting foods on one's plate. Fruits and vegetables contain thousands of health-enhancing phytochemicals. Rice, especially white rice, has almost none of them. Plus, white rice, which is what most people in India eat, has even had the fiber removed. As such, it is really just empty calories.
