**Original Question:**  
Upavasa Experience from a Sadhaka

**Full Answer:**  
To address overweight, high blood pressure, and diabetes:
1) Dairy products
2) Added oils
3) Salt

Recently, my mother has been staying with us and she has high blood pressure and diabetes. She has been eating food without salt, oil, sugar, and milk, and has started losing weight. Since we completely stopped using salt (4-5 months back), I have lost 6 kg and Nitya ji has lost 12 kg.

Without salt in our food, we can't overeat. We feel full with just 3/4 or half the amount of cooked food we used to eat before. Previously, when there was lots of salt and chili, we ate not because we were hungry but just because of salt and chili! 
And regarding weight loss, interesting point regarding the salt. When I council people on weight loss who are lacto-vegetarians, I generally focus on dairy products and added oils. As, salt doesn't itself have any calories in it. But you raise an interesting point that salty food encourages people to overeat due to their attraction/addiction to salt.

I have not seen studies/data on the point of weight loss resulting from eliminating salt from the diet. You mentioned about the weight loss you both have experienced since eliminating salt from the diet. At that time (4-5 months back), had you already eliminated dairy and added oils from your diet? That is, is it that the only thing which changed 4-5 months ago was removal of salt? If so, it is surprising for me, as I would have expected a much bigger impact of dairy and added oils on weight change than salt. If you both had already eliminated dairy and added oils quite some time ago, I would have expected the main weight loss to have occurred from that, such that there wouldn't have been scope really for further weight loss as you would have already reached your ideal weight.
Yes, Acharyaji, the only change in last 4 months that we eliminated salt! 

We first reduced salt after our last December Shivir, and then almost completely stopped taking it for the last 4 months.

Before 2022, I used to weigh around 84-85kg, but by 2022, I had reduced to 80kg, and stayed there for 2 years. However, after stopping salt, I lost another 6-7kg! Now around 73 kg. 

I completely stopped consuming dairy around 2021-22, and eliminated oil 2022-23.

After eliminating salt, two things happened:
1) eat less but feel full.
2) need less water per day (no need to drink frequently).

A nutritionist at my office suggested that excessive sodium can cause swelling and weight gain. When we consume too much salt, it triggers a process that converts the carbs we eat into a type of sugar called fructose, which can be stored as fat.
