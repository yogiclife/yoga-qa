**Original Question:**  
Where do you get your food from?

**Full Answer:**  
I buy a lot of my fruits and vegetables in bulk from a warehouse that sells to the grocery stores. So I buy boxes of bananas, 40 pounds of bananas per box. When I go to the warehouse I'll buy three boxes of bananas i.e. 120 pounds of bananas, and boxes of each other item as well-- 80 pears in a box, 80 apples in a box, 40 pounds of cauliflower, 20 pounds of broccoli, 30 pounds of spinach. Like that. Each item is in a large box, just as how they deliver them to the grocery store. And all of it is organic.
