**Original Question:**  
Clarification by acharya ji

**Full Answer:**  
Milk not only to be avoided due to ahimsa. Cows milk is not and was never good for human health. In prachiina-kala people did not know; now we know. That is the only difference. It was actually never sattvika. It was harming people in ancient days as well. Due to the saturated fat and the cholesterol it contains, as well as the dairy protein and lactose both of which many people react to. Sattvika food cannot be such that it harms people.
