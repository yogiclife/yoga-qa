**Full Answer:**  
And regarding cooked food, just to be sure the full story gets told: Regarding vegetables, it is not that I am recommending that people boil them. As we know, by so doing all the cells in the vegetable die, and most of the nutrients as well as almost all the fiber gets destroyed. It was just on the point of constipation that correction was needed; if vegetables are boiled and there is no oil used, then they will not be constipating. But the best way to eat vegetables is raw, so that they are alive.
