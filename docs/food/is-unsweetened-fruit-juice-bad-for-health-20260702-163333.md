**Original Question:**  
Is unsweetened fruit juice bad for health?

**Full Answer:**  
Re unsweetened fruit juice-- depends on what you compare it to. It is surely much better than cocacola or other artificial beverages. But if you compare it to the actual fresh raw fruits, then those juices are not good. One only has a certain amount of room in the stomach, and any time one is drinking juice-- which contains simple sugars removed from all the fiber and phytochemicals in the original fruit, one is depriving oneself of the amazing benefits of the whole fruit. Juices contain huge amounts of calories as well, and for those who have issues with weight, they are not helpful. Overall, my recommendation is for whole, raw plant foods. Juice does not come in this category as it is not whole.
