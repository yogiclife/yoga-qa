**Original Question:**  
Can cow's milk can be used instead of human mother's milk for human babies?

**Full Answer:**  
But that is wrong. Cow's milk is not adequate or safe for human babies under 1 year old, according to the American Academy of Pediatrics (AAP) and CDC. It lacks necessary nutrients like iron and Vitamin E, while containing too much protein, sodium, and potassium for infant kidneys to handle.
