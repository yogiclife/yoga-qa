**Original Question:**  
If you have any recipes for tasty raw food, that would be great too! I visited Greece last year and had tasty salads there. But the vegetables in the USA taste like plastic, compared with that. So, I wonder if we need to add some condiments to make them taste good.

**Full Answer:**  
As you might have sensed, I tend to eat very simply, just raw whole fruits and vegetables. But I do get your point about the tasteless nature of veggies and fruits in US grocery stores. For that reason I eat all organic fruits and vegetables, and those are far better. Although even there, it is best if one can get locally grown organic produce. And in the summer I do that-- i.e. I get my produce from local organic farmers. Those fruits and vegetables are wonderful.

So my approach to food is very, very simple: raw, organic fruits and vegetables--locally grown when available.

But for those who like to make things, there are lots of websites that provide great recipes. Just Google ""raw food vegan recipes"" and you'll find lots of options. Here is one good one I found just now: https://www.therawchef.com/raw-food-recipes/

When you use recipes from the web, you'll just need to remove the onion, garlic, and mushroom from their recipes, as these three items are tamasik. But there are lots of good alternatives to those ingredients which you can exchange for them.
