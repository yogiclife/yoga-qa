**Original Question:**  
Constipation, digestion, food, and nirjalaopavasa

**Full Answer:**  
Yes, it can be done in special circumstances, for example if one is quite constipated and needs to get the digestive system restarted. But otherwise, in general, the best approach is to take such food that one does not get constipated (fresh, raw fruits and vegetables), and get established in practicing nirjalopavasa on the ekadashi days. Also: If one wishes to do this practice more than twice a month in a regular way, one can do it four times per month: two ekadashii, one purnima, and one amavasya. That is what I do-- regularly, four times per month.
