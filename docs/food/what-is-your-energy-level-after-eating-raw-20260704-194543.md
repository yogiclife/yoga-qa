**Original Question:**  
What is your energy level after eating raw 

**Full Answer:**  
The nature of this question requires a subjective reply, as someone's energy level after taking a particular meal cannot be scientifically measured. But if you are asking me how I feel after eating raw food meals, that is meals of living fruits and vegetables, there is no comparison. There is so much more feeling of energy, very positive energy. I cannot express in words how it feels, truly wonderful. The stomach is filled but the body is light and the mind is sharp.

And none of the heaviness which one feels after a cooked meal. For example, raw spinach vs boiled spinach, raw carrots vs boiled carrots. I'll take the raw ones any day. The food is alive, and one gets the unique feeling of energy from the living vegetables. Try it and see!
