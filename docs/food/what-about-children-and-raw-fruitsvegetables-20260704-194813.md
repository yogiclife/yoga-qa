**Original Question:**  
What about children and raw fruits/vegetables?

**Full Answer:**  
Children can also eat and enjoy fresh fruits and vegetables. Doesn't your child like fruit? There are so many wonderful fruits which children love. And fresh raw vegetables, it may be that your child has never had before-- or rarely. But children love fresh raw fruits and vegetables. They need to be taught. Going outside into the world they see all sorts of other things-- cookies and cupcakes etc. But it is our job to teach them what is good and, just like adults, once they try it and get used to it, they will love it. (Of course, if you make rice and vegetables sometimes that is also fine-- but give them a chance to enjoy the wonderful flavors of varieties of fresh raw fruits and vegetables 😊)
