**Original Question:**  
Plant-based milk OK?

**Full Answer:**  
(a) Need to look at ingredients-- confirm that no oil, sugar or other things have been added, as often are.

(b) Soy milk and almond milk are fine. But it is important to note that coconut milk is not good. There are thousands of plants that humans do not consume. Coconut should be one of them. Coconut oil contains a huge amount of saturated fat-- that's why it is solid at room temperature. The pulp or "meat" of the coconut is also not good for same reason. So not only coconut oil, but the pulp of the coconut should also not be consumed. I am providing nutrient breakdown of coconut pulp, coconut oil, and coconut water further down in this message.
