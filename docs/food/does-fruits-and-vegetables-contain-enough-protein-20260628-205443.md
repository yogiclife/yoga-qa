**Original Question:**  
How to get protein from fruits and vegetables?

**Full Answer:**  
All fruits and vegetables provide complete protein. There is no need for beans or grains to get protein. If one eats even any single item of fruit or vegetable, the same item every day, one will get all the protein one needs. Of course it is best to eat a variety of fruits and vegetables as they each have special antioxidants and phytochemicals which are so good for you. But regarding protein, it is a non-issue as all of them contain the protein you need.
