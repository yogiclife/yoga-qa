**Full Answer:**  
I also wanted to share that sprouts are incredibly healthy, and also incredibly easy to make. Just buy some mung dal at the market, soak it overnight, then remove the water the next morning and let it grow! Rinse it with water once a day, and within two days you have beautiful sprouts. The more days you let them grow, the bigger they get. But they can be eaten any time after even just soaking them if you like, as we did at the shibiram.

They can be sprouted in official sprouting jars if you like, as shown below -- [image]

But there is really no need to purchase such jars, as the mung beans don't care at all. They'll grow just fine in any pot you put them in. See below-- [image]

So easy! And they are sweet and delicious. And so healthy! All of you should try it and see how easy it is!
