**Original Question:**  
What is Acharya ji's food program?

**Full Answer:**  
Acharya ji's food program is very simple. I simply wash the produce and eat it as-is. I'll place into my bowl three large apples, eight oranges, one bunch of spinach, a bunch of arugula, a fennel bulb with branches, a bunch of parsley, and several carrots-- and that will be my lunch. It is plenty of food! One thing to keep in mind as you do meal planning: raw food is far less calorie dense than cooked food, so one needs to eat a greater volume of food. And that is normal. It will look like a huge amount of food, but it is just a normal amount as a large amount of it is water. Raw fruits and vegetables contain a lot of water. And that water is far healthier, far more nutritious than the water one drinks from the tap or from a water purifier.
