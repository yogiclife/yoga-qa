**Original Question:**  
Also do you recommend Spirulina supplement for kids ?



**Full Answer:**  
Spirulina is very safe and healthy for people of all ages, including children. I would say no need to purchase it in pill form as a supplement though-- the spirulina powder itself is quite tasty and can be put on salads etc. Don't cook it though, it will lose all its value.
