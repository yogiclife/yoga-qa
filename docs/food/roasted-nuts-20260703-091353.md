**Original Question:**  
Roasted nuts

**Full Answer:**  
One thing I would like to mention about nuts: Nuts should be taken raw, not roasted. If the nuts are roasted it means they have ben heated to an extremely high temperature and by so doing the same thing happens to the oil they contain as what happens when people fry foods-- the oil undergoes chemical changes which make it carcinogenic. So nuts are fine to take, but they should be raw.
Most nuts sold have been roasted. If the package doesn't say which it is, it means the nuts are roasted. Nuts are always roasted unless it states on the package that they have not been.
