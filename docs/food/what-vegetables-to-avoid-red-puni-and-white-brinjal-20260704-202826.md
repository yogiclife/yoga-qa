**Original Question:**  
What vegetables to avoid: Red Puni and White Brinjal?

**Full Answer:**  
The red puni is a type of vegetable in India. I have never eaten it, and I do not know what it looks like. But it is tamasika and should be avoided.



Violet coloured brinjal is fine, it is sattvika. Just the white colored brinjal is tamasika.



In kannada it is called basale soppu / pasala keerai/ Malabar spinach
