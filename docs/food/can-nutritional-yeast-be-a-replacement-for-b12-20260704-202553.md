**Original Question:**  
Can Nutritional yeast be a replacement for B12?

**Full Answer:**  
Nutritional yeast is not at all needed. I was just explaining what the yellow powder was. It is quite tasty, and it is quite good for the body. But not at all needed-- one can get all the nutritious things it has from the fruits and vegetables themselves. One thing I'll add though, everyone does need to ensure they have a source of Vit B12 and Vit D. Nutritional yeast does contain Vit B12. General foods do not contain either of these vitamins.
