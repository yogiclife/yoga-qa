**Full Answer:**  
Ultra-processed foods should certainly be avoided as you said, but the less processed the better-- and if it is not processed at all, that is the best. That is to say, the more totally unprocessed foods one eats the better, and if one eats only unprocessed foods, that is the best. For that very reason I eat only raw fruits and vegetables. Not mostly, but only. For example, if one eats foods made from flour -- even "whole wheat flour" -- one is still losing the benefit of the whole wheat. Whole wheat and whole wheat flower are not the same thing. And then of course, we cannot eat wheat raw, it has to be cooked. And once something is cooked, it is dead and many of the phytochemicals have been destroyed. So a WFPB no SOS raw fruits and vegetables diet is the best, and anything but that represents a compromise. Every time one eats food that has been cooked, one is eating compromised food. Any time one cooks vegetables one is destroying a huge proportion of the nutrients.
