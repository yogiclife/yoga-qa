**Original Question:**  
You said the best amount to consume extracted oil is zero but you said in the sivir that you consume flaxseed oil with d3 tablets, thus the flaxseed oil is good if yes then what are those benefits of it.

**Full Answer:**  
There is something called "essential fatty acids", and these tend to be low in all people regardless of which food they eat. Flax seed oil contains them, so I used to take some flax seed oil in tiny amounts. But now I have stopped, as the conversion from the oil it contains (Alpha-Linolenic Acid -- ALA), to the needed fatty acids EPA and DHA is not so dependable. There is a better oil, extracted from algae, which contains the actual EPA and DHA. I will write something more about this another time. But the main point here is that this is not food. This is a supplement, just as vitamin D3. This oil is taken in a tiny amount, just a drop is enough.
