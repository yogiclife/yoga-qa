**Original Question:**  
Nutrition wise is it recommended to store sprouts (day 2) in refrigerator and eat it for more days (a week or more) . This will save some time.

**Full Answer:**  
Mera anubhav hai ki sprouts bahut din tak thik thak nahiin rahate hain fridge men. Kuch din tak rahenge avashy, magar ek hafte tak rahene se dekhna hoga. Roj sprouts ko pani se saf karne se zyada din tak rahenge.

Magar ek bat samjho--- samay ko bachane ki zarurat nahiin hai kyonki sprouts ko shuru karne men samay to lagta hi nahiin. Kya, tiin minat lagenge to bahut hai.
