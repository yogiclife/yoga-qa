**Original Question:**  
I have been avoiding the following foods for reasons given below. Do they need to be avoided in a yogic diet?

1. Olive — stored in brine
2. ⁠Breads — cooked at high temperatures 
3. ⁠Soy sauce — high sodium and processed

**Full Answer:**  
All good decisions. The preparation of olives for consumption results in a product which is extremely high in salt. The same applies to soy sauce. And breads are an extremely processed food, the processing of which leads to the loss of most of the nutritional benefits of the grains from which they are made. In addition to the carcinogens which are produced from cooking foods at high temperatures. 

The best approach is to focus on eating foods which are still in the form they had when they grew in the field. These are the truly proper foods for humans. Whole fresh fruits and vegetables. Grains, beans, and seeds are also very good indeed when taken in their sprouted form. Sprouts are wonderful food, extremely healthful and vitality promoting.
