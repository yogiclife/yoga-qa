**Original Question:**  
Is it correct to say "I Had to eat things which were not healthy"

**Full Answer:**  
Here we can guide people to remember that no one else puts the food in their mouth except they themselves. This is quite empowering, as it means that each person really does have full control over what they eat. So wherever they go, they can eat healthy food if they choose to. They can address the matter in a psychological way with their friends/relatives, and even use it as an opportunity to educate them.
