**Full Answer:**  
Great job, wonderful to hear you are following a vegan diet for the past one year!
The fatty liver changes and gall stones are related with your earlier way of eating/living.
Fatty liver is called primarily by a poor diet leading to obesity, Type 2 diabetes, and high cholesterol, or, in the case of those who drink alcohol, excessive alcohol consumption. Gall stones are caused similarly by excessive fat in the diet from animal products and oil added to food.

I agree with all you have expressed, that it is important to eliminate added salt, oil, and sugar from the diet. Regarding your question about the three items sugar, salt, and maida--- yes surely, very good to eliminate all three. This all comes under our concept of a "whole food, plant based diet". If you understand this name, you'll have everything you need for a proper diet. Whole food means the food should be unprocessed. Not only maida, but all flour is processed. The idea is to eat fruits, vegetables, grains, and beans as they grew in the field.

You've inquired about my own health 😊. I am 60 and have no medical problems at all. I run one hour every day, as well as doing yogasanas in the morning and evening, and yoga sadhana (meditation) twice a day for one hour each. My blood pressure and cholesterol along with all other blood tests are perfect. I have not had any need for ultrasound, but I do not have gall stones or fatty liver you can be assured. As mentioned, no medical problems at all and it'll continue like this for the rest of my life.
