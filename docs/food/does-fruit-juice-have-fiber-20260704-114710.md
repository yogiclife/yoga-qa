**Original Question:**  
Please correct my understanding. With reference to Fiber in foods, if we take Fruit juices they also lose Fiber is my understanding. 

**Full Answer:**  
That is correct-- most companies that sell fruit juice filter out all the fiber. And even if in the rare case a company allow the fibers to remain in the juice, they have been pulverized by the machine that made the juice, as a result of which most of the value of the fiber is lost. This occurs even if the juice is made at home, as any juicing machine will destroy the structural integrity of the fiber. So, best is to eat the whole fruit. No comparison.
