**Original Question:**  
The word Brahma as per nasadiya sukta in the Kara patra what is cosmic entity..does it refer to Brahma from the trinity Brahma Vishnu Maheshwara?

**Full Answer:**  
The Brahma referred to in the footnote of the karapatram is Brahma as in the Supreme Entity, Nirguna Brahma (ब्रह्म). Not Brahma in His role as creator (ब्रह्मा).
