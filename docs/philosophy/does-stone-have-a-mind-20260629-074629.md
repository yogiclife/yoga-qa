**Original Question:**  
Clarification provided by Acharya ji

**Full Answer:**  
You said that a stone does not have a developed mind. It is not merely that a stone (i.e. ksititattva) does not have a developed mind; it does not have any unit mind. There is mind present in a dormant form within it, surely. That is Cosmic Mind, which has been qualified (as stone). But only in congenial conditions, when a part of the stone is transformed into a jiiva, a single-celled living organism, then only does unit mind emerges from stone.
