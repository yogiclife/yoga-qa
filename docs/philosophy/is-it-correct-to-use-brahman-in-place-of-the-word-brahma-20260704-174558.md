**Original Question:**  
The sister who was attending the class was using the word the pronunciation Brahman (ब्राह्मन्‌) in place of the word 'brahma'

**Full Answer:**  
When this type of mistake occurs, it is important to explain the reason we do not use the pratipadikam. Otherwise if one simply says "we do not use the pratipadikam" without explaining the reason, it sounds like something dogmatic. So it will be good to tell her that it is the same pratipadikam for the pumlinga shabda and the napumsakalinga shabda, as a result of which when the pratipadikam is used, we do not know whether it refers to ब्रह्म or ब्रह्मा.
