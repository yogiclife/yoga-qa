**Original Question:**  
Mass shooters are more educated than general people, and therefore have even greater reflection of consciousness -- so why is it that they behave worse than general people who do not have such education.

**Full Answer:**  
The reply is that education is not an indication of reflected consciousness. The most "educated" are often not educated at all in the true sense of the term education. Such book knowledge is not what we in yoga call education. Those so-called gyaniis are crude, all they think about is money, power, name, fame.
