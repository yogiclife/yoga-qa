**Full Answer:**  
So regarding her question as to which is dominant, the reply is: shaktiH sA shivasya shaktiH. Shakti is merely a force, and she is completely obedient, 100% engaged in following the directions she has received from Shiva. Prakrti does not have a mind or brain of her own to be making "decisions" per se. Yes, She does make the arrangements for the fruition of samskara, but that does not involve "decisions" as such; she is merely executing the orders and guidelines she has received from Purusa. Prakrti has received an algorithm from Purusa and She is dedicated for executing it. She is the executor of the algorithm to be sure-- but Purusa is the giver of that algorithm. Purusa is the Master Mind behind everything in the universe. It is similar to the relation between a human being and a computer. It is true that the computer is performing huge work and may appear to be engaged in intricate decision-making all along the way. But actually the computer has no mind and cannot "think" per se. It merely executes what it is designed to do. Similar is the case with Prakrti. She is merely a force; she has no mind and no desires and is simply executing the works she has been ordered to do. The mastermind is Purusa. So X ji should understand that Prakrti is not dominant; Purusa is the dominant factor at all times and at all phases of the srsti, as it is His instructions that are being followed.
