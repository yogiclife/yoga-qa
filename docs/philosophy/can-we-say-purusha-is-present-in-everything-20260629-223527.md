**Original Question:**  
Clarification provided by Acharya ji

**Full Answer:**  
Just, rather than saying the Purusa is "Present in all forms (matter + mind + life)", in place of "present in", better to say "the only ingredient in". Everything is made of purusa and nothing else. By saying "present in" it sounds like it is one among other ingredients. When one bakes a cake, one uses flour, water, sugar, milk, etc. Similarly, purusa is one of the ingredients in all things. But as we know, it is not at all like that. So take care to word it clearly.
