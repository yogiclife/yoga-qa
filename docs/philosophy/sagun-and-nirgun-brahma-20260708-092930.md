**Original Question:**  
A question was there about saguna brahma Sadhana.. since we are all with attributes so why is it not ideal to worship the Purusha with attribute also? Why is that everyone has to try for the Nirguna Purusha Sadhana? Is there any way through the Saguna process Sadhana We ultimately end up in Nirguna Sadhana? Is there any way to merge? Like starting with Saguna and ending with Nirguna.

**Full Answer:**  
One point in your explanation to her - you said to her that the difference is that Saguna Brahma has a rupa or form, in contrast to Nirguna Brahma which is beyond rupa and beyond guna. Actually, neither Saguna Brahma nor Nirguna Brahma has form. Saguna Brahma is Cosmic Mind. As we know, the human being has a brain and a mind. The brain has a form, but the mind does not. It is suksma, it has no tanmatras. Cosmic Mind is also suksma. Indeed, unit mind is just a part of Cosmic Mind. If you tell her that Saguna Brahma has form, then she will think this refers to the various gods and godesses she worships, the various artist renditions of murtis made of stone, clay, and wood. So it is important to clarify that neither Saguna Brahma nor Nirguna Brahma has form. The former is Cosmic Mind, and the latter is Shuddha Purusa. Both are beyond form.
