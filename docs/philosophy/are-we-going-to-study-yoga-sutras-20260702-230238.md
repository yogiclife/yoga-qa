**Original Question:**  
(1) Which books are we following?
(2) Are we going to study yoga sutras?


**Full Answer:**  
1) We will be using karapatrams on the classical rajadhiraja yoga.
2) This class is designed around how to live a yoga nisth jiivan. We will teach you the philosophy underlying yoga as well to be sure, but yoga is in its essence a practical science. 1% theory and 99% theory. We will be very focused on how to apply yoga in one's life, how to live a true human life. Just reading books has no benefit for anyone.
Tell them that by implementing the yogic teachings to be presented here in one's life-- yogic food, nirjalopavasa, asanas, sadhana, vyapak shaoca, their life will be completely revolutionized. And as many of them may be older and on various medications, you can tell them that by implementing these teachings in their life they will become far, far more healthy and will be able to get off of their blood pressure medicines, diabetes medications, and so many other conditions will be cured.
