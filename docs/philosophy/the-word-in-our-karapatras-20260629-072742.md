**Original Question:**  
Clarification provided by Acharya ji

**Full Answer:**  
In the karapatram the word jinana appears. The devanagari is ज्ञान (not जिज्ञान) ।  The breakdown is as follows: ज्‌ (j) ञ्‌ (in) आ (a) न  (n)।
