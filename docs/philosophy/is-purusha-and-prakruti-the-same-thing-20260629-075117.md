**Original Question:**  
Clarification by Acharya ji

**Full Answer:**  
One student asked you whether purusa and Parama Purusa are the same thing, and you replied that they are. While related, these are not synonymous terms. Purusa is the substrate, whereas Parama Purusa is the Central Controller of all purusa and everything that is made from it.
