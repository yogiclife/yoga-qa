**Original Question:**  
Clarification by Acharya ji

**Full Answer:**  
 Prakrti = not a part of purusa. I think you understand this point, but I am just mentioning this as the way in which we express ourselves is important. So yes, Prakrti is within purusa (nothing is outside of purusa), but it is not a part of purusa. Prakrti is a separate entity, and purusa is a separate entity. Similar to the way in which when light travels through space we do not say that light is "part" of space (they are both different tattvas-- agni tattva and akasha tattva). In the same way, Prakrti is a force that is found "within" the infinite ocean of purusa, but is not a "part" of it.
