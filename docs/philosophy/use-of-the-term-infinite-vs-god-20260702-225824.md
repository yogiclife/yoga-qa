**Original Question:**  
Namaskar to all, I took the session and I discussed definition of yoga but I only used the term infinite, but a person was asking union with god is not mentioned, I said we will discuss it in the future session talk more about God. So any suggestions how to handle this question like in how much detail the answer should be given? 

**Full Answer:**  
The reason for using the word "infinite" and not "God" or any other similar term like "dev", "devata", Brahma, etc is that people have been raised in dogmatic environments and so have dogmatic associations with all these terms since their childhood. Because of that, using the term "The Infinite", then slowly over several sessions introducing "The Infinite Entity", and ultimately "Supreme Consciousness", keeps them on the track of the proper ideation. People cannot misunderstand "The Infinite" to mean a statue or idol. The meaning of the word "Infinite" in itself defines what we are talking about, so that the wrong idea or wrong bhav cannot emerge in people's minds.
