**Original Question:**  
how it is that we are animals in human form, rather it seems we become worse than animals. He gave the mass shooter example.

**Full Answer:**  
The reason is that humans have an infinite desire, whether they are aware of it or not-- and when they apply their infinite desire in the field of pashu dharma, then it makes them abuse that pashu dharma, which is animals themselves never do as animals have limited desire. So animals only take the amount of food they need, only sleep as much as they need, etc. But humans misuse the animal dharma due to their having an infinite desire
