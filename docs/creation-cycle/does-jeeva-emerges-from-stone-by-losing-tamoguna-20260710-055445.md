**Original Question:**  
can you check if my understanding is correct? When jiva emerges from stone, it starts by de-crudifying, losing some tamoguna and acquiring sattva to form the unit mind. Initially, at the stone stage, everything is in the cosmic chitta. When some tamoguna is lost to reach the chitta stage, this unit chitta is still in the cosmic chitta. When more gunas are lost in some parts, then the resulting unit ahamtattva becomes a part of the cosmic ahamtattva. After still more de-qualification, the unit mahattavva emerges and is in the cosmic mahattattva. It then gets linked to a part of the cosmic consciousness that had never been qualified, which is the jeevatma.



**Full Answer:**  
So when the jiiva emerges from stone it has a mind which is dominated by citta, but also contains ahamtattva and mahattattva in dormant form. The factor that leads to the emergence of the jiiva from ksititattva is the shift to the dominance of sattvaguna, rather than the "loss of tamoguna". Rather than talk about "loss of gunas" or "dequalification" it will be more helpful to describe this process as one of development, as mind is present even in ksititattva in dormant form-- so now by the shift to dominance of sattvaguna of Prakrti, that dormant mind present in ksititattva develops and, along with support from the dominance of the interial forces (known as prANAH) within ksititattva which had developed earlier in response to the pressure of tamoguna, result in the emergence of a jiiva with an active unit mind. From there going forward, through clash and cohesion, i.e. through struggle for survival, the mind gradually develops, with the ahamtattva becoming active when with rebirth the jiiva reaches the animal stage, and then later the mahattattva gradually becomes active as well. But at the very moment of emergence of the jiiva from ksitatattva, from that very moment there is jiivatma linked with the unit mind. An yes, that unit mind is nothing but a portion of the Cosmic Mind which had been created from 1:00 to 3:00 and then got gradually qualified from 3:00 to 6:00. And the linked jiivatma is nothing but a portion of Paramatma which has been present in unqualified form throughout.
