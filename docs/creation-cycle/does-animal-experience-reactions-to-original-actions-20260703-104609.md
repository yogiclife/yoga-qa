**Original Question:**  
Does Animal experience reactions to original actions?

**Full Answer:**  
It is true that, unlike humans, animals cannot do any original actions because they do not have free will. But that does not mean they cannot experience the reactions to original actions which they have done when they were humans. Indeed they do experience those reactions. All sorts of crazy, negative, outrageous things which they do as human beings, as a result of which their mind degenerates, when they get born in their next life as stones/plants/animals they go through all sorts of suffering and difficulties which are the results of the actions which they took as humans. They may not burn all the samskaras which they created as humans, but they burn many of them. Such that the next time they take birth as humans, they will be very different from how they were the last time they died as humans. They may or not may not be ready to live as humans in human form, but they will be better than they were before-- because many of the samskaras they have burnt.
