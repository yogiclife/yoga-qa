**Original Question:**  
Sound travelling in a vacuum: Is Akashatattva and outer space the same thing?

**Full Answer:**  
Akashatattva and outer space are indeed the same thing. Outer space is the place where we get akashatattva without any of the other four tattvas. The fact that sound travels through the space in our house is not evidence that sound travels through akashatattva. Yes, there is akashatattva in the house, but there is also vayutattva. The sound is traveling through the house via vayutattva, not via akashatattva. To show that sound travels through akashatattva, we would need to show that sound can travel through outer space, as that is the place were we get akashatattva without vayutattva.

There are two solutions which can be considered-- one is that shabda is referring to onkaradhvani as it pertains to travel through akashatattva. The second is the concept  i.e. that shabda is referring to information and therefore that it travels through akashatattva in various other formats.
