**Original Question:**  
Is there a reflection of consciouness in ksititattva at 6:00?

**Full Answer:**  
But that is not the case; there is no reflection of consciousness in ksititattva. That requires manifestation of mind, which is only present in jiivas. The mind is the reflecting plate. It is true that the stone is made only of purusa, yes certainly. But it cannot reflect consciousness as it does not have mind manifest within it. Mind is there, but it is not manifest. Under anukul paristhiti, we get manifestation of anumana as the jiiva emerges from stone. Then reflection of consciousness begins.
