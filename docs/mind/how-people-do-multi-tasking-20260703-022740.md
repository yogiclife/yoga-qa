**Original Question:**  
How people do multi-tasking or Ahamtattva can do multiple task?

**Full Answer:**  
Mind can only take the shape of one thing at a time. As we have discussed many times in our satsang sessions, there is no such thing as multi-tasking. Rather it just looks like that, but is actually a rapid sequence of doing one task after the next.
