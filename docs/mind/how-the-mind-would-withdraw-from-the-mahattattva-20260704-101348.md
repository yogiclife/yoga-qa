**Original Question:**  
I am wondering how the mind would withdraw from the mahattattva. It looks like a description of nirvikalpa samadhi. From what I remember, the anu-mind is suspended in both savikalpa and nirvikalpa samadhi. So, the mind will no longer be concentrating on anything, isn't it?

**Full Answer:**  
The mahattattva is present, but ahamtattva and citta are not, hence the mahattattva is unaffected by their usual vacillation. The mind is fully focused on the Ocean of Consciousness and hence in a state of uninterrupted concentration.
