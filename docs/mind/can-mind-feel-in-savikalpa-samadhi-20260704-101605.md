**Original Question:**  
Paragraph 9 of the same handout mentions, 'Concentration of mind is only Savikalpa Samadhi where the only feeling that remains is I am that.' If the mind is suspended during savikalpa samadhi, then is the feeling something that arises in jeevatma. I assume the mind cannot feel because it is suspended.

**Full Answer:**  
In Savikalpa Samadhi the mahattattva is present and the mind has become what it is thinking about in sadhana, and hence has taken on the characteristics of its visaya and lost all vacillation. This is the concentrated state of the mind.
