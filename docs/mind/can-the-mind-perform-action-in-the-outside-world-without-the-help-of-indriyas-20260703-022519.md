**Original Question:**  
Can the mind perform action in the outside world without the help of Indriyas?

**Full Answer:**  
As the kulakundalinii rises, the mind gets access to occult powers/alaokika shakti/siddhis. This is because Parama Purusa is sarvashaktiman and sarvagya. And He does and knows everything without needing any indriyas, because for Him, everything is within His mind. As the mind expands on the path of spirituality, the anumana gradually becomes connected with Bhuma mana and thereby gets access to some of these powers which for Parama Purusa are just natural as everything is within His mind. But the important thing here is that for a sadhaka such knowledge or such power is of no value whatsoever as the use of such alaokika shakti serves only to degenerate the mind as it makes the user of such tools feel that his small I-feeling is something very special and glorified. So in response to your question, yes such magicians and their magic do exist but they are degenerate persons and their magic is of zero interest for a sadhaka.
