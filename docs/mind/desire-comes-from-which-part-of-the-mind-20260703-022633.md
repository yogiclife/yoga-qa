**Original Question:**  
1. Desire come from which entity? is it come from Ahamtattva or Mahattatva. In the discourse there is a line “... when ahamtattva wants to hear something…” does it mean Ahamttva have the desire? 

**Full Answer:**  
The desire to do various works resides in ahamtattva. But it depends on a developed mahattattva. Without the latter, aham-tattva is motivated only by inborn instincts.
