**Original Question:**  
Tanmatras

**Full Answer:**  
At the very end, when Bhagini asked about tanmatras, it was mentioned that the indriyas themselves are suksma. The indriyas are located in the brain, which is itself indriya-grahya. The entire process of sense perception up to the level of the brain involves tools-- the eyes, the nerves, and the brain all of which are themselves indriya-grahya. It is only after that when the message is passed from the brain to the mind (citta), that we reach a realm which is not indriya-grahya.
