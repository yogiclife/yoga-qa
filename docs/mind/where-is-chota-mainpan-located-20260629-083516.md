**Original Question:**  
Clarification provided by Acharya ji

**Full Answer:**  
chote main ko lekar-- kya hai, man men kahan hai. Unka prashn utana spast nahiin tha kyonki "chote main" jab puchti hain to "mainpan" ko lekar puch rahi hain, ya phir "ahamkar" ko lekar puch rahi hain? Chote main ka mainpan sthit hai mahattattv men; chote main ka ahamkar sthit hai ahamtattva men.
