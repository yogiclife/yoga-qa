**Original Question:**  
Mahodaya, you mentioned taraka brahma being born in the world as Shiva. Can you clarify what incident this relates to? Krishna’s story, of course, is well known.

**Full Answer:**  
Just as Lord Krsna took birth on this earth 3500 years ago to bring dharma to its proper place, so also Lord Shiva did 7000 years ago. The phases of society were very different, but the crisis demanding the avirbhava of mahasambhuti was at its root the same-- society was stuck in the mud and unable to raise manava dharma to its proper place.

We will be reading more about the details of both mahasambhutis quite a bit as we move ahead in our satsang together.
