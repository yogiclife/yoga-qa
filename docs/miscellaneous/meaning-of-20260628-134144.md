**Original Question:**  
Clarification provided by acharya ji.

**Full Answer:**  
And today we have another new name, which is for Sabya ji. His spiritual name is सर्वज्ञ .  Being all-knowing, having access to all the knowledge of the universe, is a natural quality of that Infinite Entity, for the entire universe is within His mind. And each and every one of us, as part of that Infinite Entity, has that very potential within us! The unconscious mind within each and every human being is linked to and has access to all the knowledge of the universe. The jiivatma within each person is part of that very Paramatma. And the unit mind of each and every person is part of that Cosmic Mind. So Sabya ji's new spiritual name, सर्वज्ञ, carries this very meaning. It vibrates and reminds that you are Brahma, that All-knowing Entity! You are one with Him, and as such you have all of that potential within!
