**Original Question:**  
Significance of Tarpana/Shradh (rituals after death ). Does it have any effect on the journey of the Jiv atman of the deceased person?

**Full Answer:**  
The rituals that people engage in after a person's death have zero effect on the journey of the man (मन) and jiivatma of the deceased person. Those things are strictly for the comfort of those who are still alive i.e. the family members. These are just imaginary activities by which the family members get some soothing comfort. But it is all fake and is better left.
