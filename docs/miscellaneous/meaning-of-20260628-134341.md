**Original Question:**  
Clarification provided by acharya ji.

**Full Answer:**  
It is with great pleasure that I announce our next spiritual name. Rohit ji has requested one, and his new name is 'रसेश्वर'. 

'रसेश्वर' means "Controller of Cosmic Flow." That is, Controller of Universal Flow, Controller of Eternal Flow. The controller of that flow is Parama Purusa. 

विश्व का जो धारा-प्रवाह है, उसका नियन्त्रक-- माने परमपुरुष ही | रसस्य ईश्वरः, रस के ईश्वर, इसी अर्थ में रसेश्वर । 

With beautiful enthusiasm, when I gave him the name, he replied-- "Bohot hi sundar hai".

So, going forward, Rohit ji will be known as Raseshvar.
