**Original Question:**  
Clarification provided by acharya ji.

**Full Answer:**  
Having a spiritual name is deeply meaningful. When one has a name which means Parama Purusa, then every time the name is used it reminds both those speaking the name and those hearing it, that this person is the gunanvita, gunayukta manifestation of Parama Purusa Himself. 

Two more of our shibiram participants have requested and received spiritual names, Shubham ji and Sabya ji. Today I am announcing the new name of Shubham ji, and tomorrow, that of Sabya ji. 

Shubham ji's new spiritual name is शुभ्रज्योति | In explaining the meaning and significance I sent her the below, and this is applicable for all.

हिरण्मय कोष के ऊपर में (अर्थात्‌ जहाँ मन का अन्त हो जाता है, वहाँ) किसकी अवस्थिति है ? शुभ्र ज्योति की | किसकी शुभ्र ज्योति ? परमपुरुष की | यह शुभ्र ज्योति बहुत ही शुभ्र, देखने में बहुत ही अच्छा, बहुत ही सुन्दर | साधना करो, आसानी से देख लोगी | मानव जीवन धन्य हो जाएगा अगर उस ज्योति को देख लिए |
