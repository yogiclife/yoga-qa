**Original Question:**

**Full Answer:**  
The place we reserve for the yoga shibiram each year is so serene and beautiful. The true beauty of the experience though can only be had during sadhana. Millions and millions of people travel the world each year and go to beautiful places in search of mental peace. But in the absence of sadhana, none of them get the peace they seek. For mental peace is an inner experience which cannot be gotten by physically going somewhere. Why not join us next year? Start planning now so that you can experience that eternal peace...then it will really be the perfect place to be. 😊🙏
