**Full Answer:**  
There are two types of disease: (1) diseases of the brain; (2) diseases of the mind.
Autism is a disease of the brain, not of the mind. In autism, there are defects in the brain due to which it does not function properly. If they are bullied and as a result they are depressed, in that state the mind is also involved, means this sort of depression is a mental disease.
