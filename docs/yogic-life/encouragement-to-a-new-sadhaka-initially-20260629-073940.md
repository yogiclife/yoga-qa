**Original Question:**  
Encouragement to a new sadhaka initially

**Full Answer:**  
Encouraging students and guiding them in upavasa, it will need similar encouragement and guidance going forward, each time ekadashi is approaching. Otherwise the easy thing for them is to never do it, never even try it. So they will need ongoing pushing, nudging, and encouragement. 

These initial months of classes are in my view the most important as they get the students established in fundamental practices in the yogic life. If they sincerely follow these practices, it will change their mindset. And this change in mindset makes them ready to understand and adopt the spiritual outlook, including an understanding of their place in the srsti cakra--where they have come from and where they are going. And if they come that far i.e. adopt the various practices and learn the spiritual outlook given in the karapatrams, then they are ready to come to the yoga shibiram and get involved in sadhana.
