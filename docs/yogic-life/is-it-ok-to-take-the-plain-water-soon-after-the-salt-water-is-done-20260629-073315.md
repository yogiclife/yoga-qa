**Original Question:**  
Is it ok to take the plain water soon after the salt water is done?

**Full Answer:**  
 I thought it would dilute the salt water, and the diluted salt water may get absorbed by the body.
 Everyone's digestive tract may move at slightly different rates, so 15 min/L or 20 min/L either is good. This number i.e. 15-20 min per liter can be a helpful number to share with students as a liter is a standard amount. In place of saying 2 min/glass.
 By the time one has drunk 4 liters of salt water, I find that things are moving along fairly quickly such that there is no sensation of bloating at all, and it doesn't feel like there is any water remaining in the stomach. So I usually continue at the same pace i.e. the 5th liter, which is plain water, I take in the 5th 15 minute time period. And it works well for me-- for the first hour there is no urination, and after drinking the 5th liter, urination begins. Plus, the 1-2 liters of plain water do also help to complete the job of cleaning by pushing any remaining elements which may remain in the gut, out. If you would like to give some time before starting to drink plain water though, and it works well for you, then that is also surely fine.
