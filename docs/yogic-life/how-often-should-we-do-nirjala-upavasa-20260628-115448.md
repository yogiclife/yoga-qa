**Full Answer:**  
Today I am doing purnima nirjalopavasa. I practice nirjalopavasa four times per month-- ekadashii x 2, amavasya, purnima. For those just starting out, doing it 2x/month on ekadashii is very good. And you have the flexibility to choose among the day of ekadashii, the day prior, or the day after according to what suits your schedule best. The actual day of ekadashii is best if it suits you. It should be a day on which you do not have too busy a schedule and if possible can remain at home to do more sadhana. But I will add that it is not a day on which you will not have any energy. I have done many nirjalopavasas working in the emergency room which is quite strenuous, and there is no problem at all. Today I did not work at the hospital, but I did go for a full one hour off-road run in the forest -- beautiful!
