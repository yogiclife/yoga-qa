**Full Answer:**  
One point at the very end, one sister was asking about walking as exercise, whether to do multiple short 20-minute bouts or one longer bout of one hour, and you correctly replied that one hour will be better. The only thing I would like to add here is that sisters in India think that running is only for brothers; sisters should walk. This is a bogus idea, and we should break it and throw it in the garbage. Let's encourage all the sisters to run-- they can do it from the very beginning. Otherwise, what they are thinking of as a "morning walk", that is not sufficient. To make it more clear to them what we are talking about regarding sweating, the exercise should be such that they absolutely need to bathe when they are finished. None of this going out to gossip with their friends for a few minutes, dressed in a sarii, consider it their "morning walk", then come home and cook breakfast. They should return home soaking wet, out of breath, and need to bathe. Everyone can run. They should wear sweat pants and running shoes when they go out, and take it seriously. It may be that they could only run for 10 minutes when they begin, but gradually it will extend to longer and longer periods of time. They should be out of breath and soaked in sweat when they get home.
