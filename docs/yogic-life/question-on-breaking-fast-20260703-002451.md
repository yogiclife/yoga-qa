I was fasting on Monday, Ekadasi day as per your instructions for 34 hrs. I felt more energetic on dwadasi day morning... I broke the fast by drinking 1 litre water with 1tsp salt and half lemon juice.. by about 5am.After 2 hrs, I passed very little quantity of liquid motion (total about 30ml.. in two times)After that nothing came out, not even clear water! **Full Answer:**  
- No one will get cleaning by drinking just one liter of salt water. It is not proper to tell them to drink one liter of salt water and then wait to see if cleaning happens or not. This will not work for anyone.
- Everyone should drink one liter of salt water EVERY 30 MINUTES.
- They should continue drinking one liter of salt water EVERY 30 MINUTES even after cleaning begins, until the process is complete i.e. what comes into the toilet has no color and no smell.
- After that they should then drink one liter of water without any salt in it.
- With regard to the amount of salt needed in each liter, it is a balance between added too little (in which case the person will urinate instead of defecate), and too much (in which case it will cause nausea and perhaps vomiting as well). Each person needs to learn through experimentation how much salt they need in each liter.
- I have a pdf on my class page which guides how to break fast. You should post it on your page as well (the page where you post your recordings on our website).
Most people will require minimum THREE LITERS of salt water. There may be some few who may reach to the goal with two liters of salt water. No one will complete the job with just one liter. I drink four liters every time, and then a fifth which is pure water.
