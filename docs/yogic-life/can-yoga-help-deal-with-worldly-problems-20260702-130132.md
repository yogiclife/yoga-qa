**Original Question:**  
One of my students sent the following message related to the reason for taking yoga. "Recently there was a massive theft in my house and I felt gang of thieves are after us.Though I changed the house I donot have the peace of mind which I had before.I wish to meditate more and keep my mind on the Eternal" I was wondering how I should respond. Shall I say that yoga will certainly yield peace of mind, but that he/she also needs to take suitable actions in the material world, like contacting the police?

**Full Answer:**  
Yes, certainly, what you suggested is correct-- both approaches are needed.
