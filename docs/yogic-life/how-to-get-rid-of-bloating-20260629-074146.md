**Original Question:**  
How to get rid of bloating?

**Full Answer:**  
Nice experience reported by the first sister who was able to follow the entire nirjalopavasa as well as the breaking prakrya. One thing, she said she was hoping it would get rid of her bloating problem, but said it didn't achieve that as after she broke fast and started taking her "regular food", she again got the feeling of bloating. So two things here: (1) Did she get full breaking, in the end was it colorless, like water? That is important. And (2) she started feeling bloating when she took her "regular food". Well, what is her "regular food"? If it is with oil, salt, paneer etc, and if it is mostly rice with a bit of fried vegetable, then that is the cause for her bloating. She needs to learn what to eat. No doubt you have already explained it, but when she says she is getting "bloating", then this is an opportunity for us to explain it again. Emphasis on raw fruits and vegetables. People will not experience bloating when they eat these foods. If she wants cooked food, then boiled only, and take mostly vegetable, minimum rice.
