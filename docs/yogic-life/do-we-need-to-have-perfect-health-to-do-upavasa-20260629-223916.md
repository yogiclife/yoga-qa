**Original Question:**  
Clarification provided by Acharya ji

**Full Answer:**  
One brother proclaimed that one needs to be in a perfect state of health to do nirjalopavasa. This is not at all true. Rather the reverse: Nirjalopavasa is a healer of disease. Chronic disease gets healed more rapidly when nirjalopavasa is included in one's yogic life practices.
