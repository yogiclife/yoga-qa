**Original Question:**  
Mahodaya, one of my new students is looking for a solution for chronic headache. Is that something the yogic diet can solve? 

**Full Answer:**  
It depends on the cause of the headache. Chronic headache has many causes: There are serious causes like brain tumor, subdural hematoma (bleeding inside the head), dangerously high blood pressure, hydrocephalus-- causes such as these first need to be ruled out or excluded. Once one knows it is not like-threatening, then too there is a long list of general causes such as migraine, TMJ, visual issues, emotional issues, tension, and so many other causes. So, first the case of the headache needs to be assessed. Then only one can assess how responsive it will be to lifestyle change and yoga. The reality is that in most cases, the headache is not due to a life-threatening cause and in most cases it will be very responsive to the yogic life. But first one needs to assess the cause, as discussed above.
