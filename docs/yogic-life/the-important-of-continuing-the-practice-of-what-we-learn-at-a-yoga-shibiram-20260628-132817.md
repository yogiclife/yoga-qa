**Full Answer:**  
I want to encourage all of you, the most valuable thing to take home with you from the yoga shibiram is implementation of the practices you've learned in your daily life.  And this is the time to do it-- the phase immediately following the shibiram experience. If you implement the yogic practices now, they will become part of your life, invigorate you, give you fresh energy, and endless inspiration to move toward becoming one with that Supreme Entity. This is life-changing. And if you do not implement the yogic practices, then they will just become forgotten things of the past like so many other things which came and went in life. And then all you learnt will have been of zero value. So if you haven't started WFPB food with no SOS or onion/garlic, now is the time! Do we live to eat, or do we eat to live? The latter! Start using food as fuel, and maximize your intake of fresh, raw fruits and vegetables. Do your sadhana and asanas morning and evening! Exercise/go running every day! Do vyapaka shaoca before sadhana, meals, and sleep! And practice nirjalopavasa on Ekadashii. Now is the time to invigorate your life, get off any allopathic medications, live to 100 healthy and full of energy, and move toward becoming one with Parama Purusha. We have spent ages living as animals in human form: now is the time to leave all that behind, and start your new life as a true human being. 🙏 ‎
