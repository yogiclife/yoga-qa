**Original Question:**  
Does drinking salt water to break a fast increase blood pressure?

**Full Answer:**  
Experience of one teacher -- since I had high blood pressure — much higher than his starting point. My blood pressure slowly declined, but I did experience higher blood pressure for a couple of days after upavasa each time. But with overall trend being downward, this seemed ok. Even now, my blood pressure goes higher for a couple of days after upavasa. But the high levels are still normal. 

I used to have a quarter to half a teaspoon of salt each day. No meat or dairy though. 

Incredentally, when I went to ER about a month back a couple of days after upavasa, my blood pressure was around 170/120, which is apparently close to dangerous. No cause was identified, but the doctor said being in a medical facility itself raises blood pressure. It became normal a few days later.
