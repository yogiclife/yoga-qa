**Original Question:**  
And Thank you, but what happens in a year is anybody's guess. And the first chance I get, I would like to stay at benaras for a while. For sadhana only. [The context is acharya ji encouraging a student to attend the yoga shibiram the next year.]

**Full Answer:**  
It is surely true that we cannot know or predict all the events of the coming year. But as human beings we do have free will, as a result of which, unlike the case of animals, planning is not beyond our capacity. If someone takes a firm determination and gives something high priority, in most cases they can do it. It really depends on the value and importance a person gives to something. If the whole life passes by and a person never learns yogasadhana, and says they "did not have time", what it really means is they did not care about or want to learn it. There is time in life for the most important things: It all depends on what one considers to be most important.
