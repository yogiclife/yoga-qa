**Full Answer:**  
This does sound like sciatica. There are three asanas which I recommend for this.

1. Bhujaungasana (cobra pose) - inhale, hold breath 8 seconds as go into pose, exhale on release. Repeat 8 times.
2. Table pose - go onto hands and knees. Extend right hand and left leg, hold for 30 seconds, normal breathing, then do other side. Repeat both sides twice.
3. Shalabhasana - extend and lift right arm, left leg, inhale, hold breath 8 seconds as go into pose, exhale on release. Repeat each side 4 - 6 times.
