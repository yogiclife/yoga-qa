**Original Question:**  
Clarification given by Acharya ji

**Full Answer:**  
The brother who said he wanted to do upavasa on the weekend for his first time, that is fine. What you explained to him about the mental determination and the mental approach for getting success, that is excellent. But for the first time, if he likes, then if ekadashii is not coming on a good day for him, neither day before or after, then he can do purnima/amavasya the first time. And if not that, then any weekend is fine for the first time-- main thing is they should do it. Any day it can be done the first time, even if it is not the ekadashii day.
