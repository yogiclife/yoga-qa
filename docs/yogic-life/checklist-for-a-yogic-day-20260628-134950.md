**Full Answer:**  
I hope you are all doing well!
I want to encourage each of you to maintain your yogic practices, most importantly sadhana. The sadhana (meditation) which you learned at the shivir is the center of human life. The reason for that is that Parama Purusa is the goal of life, He is everything in life. And the way to reach Him is by going inward through sadhana. Sadhana is the way, and Parama Purusa is the goal.

So set the following as a checklist to be followed and completed daily:
- sadhana 2x/day
- asanas 2x/day (with a timer, the dances and asanas which we were doing together daily at the shivir.)
- vyapaka shaoca (before sadhana, meals, and sleep)
- WFPB sattvika ahara
- Exercise (such as running)
- Nirjalopavasa (on ekadashi, with shaoca prakriya the following day)
