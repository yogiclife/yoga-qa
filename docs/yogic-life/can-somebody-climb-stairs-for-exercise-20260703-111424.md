**Original Question:**  
Can somebody climb stairs for exercise?

**Full Answer:**  
Regarding the question about stairs: Whatever kind of exercise people do—whatever they like and enjoy—is perfectly fine. I don't know if she was talking specifically about stairs, but if it is more convenient in her life to go up and down, up and down the stairs compared to going outside for a run, and she is doing it intensely so that she breaks a sweat, then that is also completely fine.
