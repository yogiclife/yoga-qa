**Original Question:**  
Why nirjalopavasa?

**Full Answer:**  
 It is the only way to enter a fasting state. All those other so-called upavasas are fake. People taking water, buttermilk, dates-- as soon as one takes any of these, the body is not in a fasting state.

- Hearing all their talk, you mentioned they should avoid taking water or dates in order to enter a "complete" fasting state. But here it will be better to leave out the word "complete". Otherwise, hearing this they will think that they can drink water and eat dates and be in a "partial" fasting state. But there is no such thing as a partial fasting state. One is either in it or one is not. It is all or none.
